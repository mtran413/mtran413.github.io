Hello! I’m Michelle.

I am a senior at Pomona College studying Cognitive Science.

I am a user experience researcher passionate about creating accessible and intuitive experiences for users, especially for underrepresented groups such as first-generation low-income identities and English as second language users.

Being a first-generation low-income student and having parents who speak English as their second language made me passionate about making technology more accessible and beneficial for these groups, as well as other underrepresented communities.
